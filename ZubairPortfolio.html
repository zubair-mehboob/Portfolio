Successful developers don’t bring their egos to work. No matter how good you are at what you do, you’re not always going to be right. Other team members might have constructive criticism during code review, or your code might have bugs that you only find out about after shipping the product. When this happens, you have to be OK with other people pointing out and correcting your errors. That’s why humility is such an important quality for developers to have.
